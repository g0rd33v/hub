The idea

Think of it as an airport.

A big airport is where everything passes through. People fly in, fly out, change planes. Cargo moves alongside passengers. Different airlines share the same runways. Information boards keep everyone oriented. It works because everything connects in one place — but nothing is forced through any single path.

Hub is that, for everything you have online.

Your bot, your channel, your site, your app, your ChatGPT account, your Claude account, your APIs — they all plug into Hub. Information flows between them: a message from your bot reaches your group, a question from your site goes to ChatGPT, a result from Claude goes back to your customers. You set the routes. Hub runs the traffic.

And because Hub starts inside Telegram, you manage all of this from a chat. No dashboards, no admin panels, no separate logins.
