What Hub does for you

A dozen things, all running quietly in the background.

Like the smart home that locks the door when you leave and turns the lights down at night. Hub does these for your digital life — none of them require a setup wizard.

SIGN IN & ACCESS

One link, the right access

No passwords. Hub gives you a private link for everything you own — a project, a contributor seat, the whole server. Click the link, you're in.

PROJECT IN ONE STEP

Paste a bot token, done

Get a token from BotFather, paste it into Hub. You instantly have a project, a live URL, and a working bot. No setup wizards.

3 WAYS TO RUN A BOT

From zero-config to full code

Drop nothing — bot answers with a clean welcome. Drop a config file — replies and buttons work. Drop code — your bot does anything you write. Pick the level you need.

VERSIONS

Every change saved forever

Built on real Git underneath. Every edit is a version. Roll back anytime. View any past version by URL. Nothing is ever lost.

STORAGE

Built-in database

Every project gets a key-value store. Save user data, bot state, leaderboards — without setting up a database. Ten megabytes per project, free.

SCHEDULES

Run things on a timer

Daily reminders. Hourly checks. Weekly digests. Add a schedule, Hub fires it. Works for any bot mode.

COLLABORATORS

Invite without giving the keys

Hand out contributor links. They edit in their own branch. You review, you merge — or you don't. Your live project stays safe.

GITHUB

Auto-sync to your repo

Connect any GitHub repo. Every Hub commit pushes there automatically. Or pull from GitHub — Hub redeploys on its own.

BROADCAST

Reach all your users at once

People who tap /start on your bot become subscribers automatically. Send one message, reach all of them. No lists to manage.

ANALYTICS

See who's using it

Active users, languages, peak hours, message types. Per project, always live, no setup. Your data stays yours.

WEBHOOKS

Plug Hub into your own server

Want to handle bot updates yourself? Set a webhook URL — Hub forwards every message to your server, with full delivery logs.

SAFETY NET

Daily backups, automatic

Every day, all your projects are backed up. Stored on Hub, mirrored to two more servers. If something breaks, nothing is lost.

The idea

Think of it as a smart home.

A smart home doesn't replace your lights, your speakers, your locks. It connects what you already own and makes them work together. You leave the house — the door locks, the lights dim, the music stops. You didn't program any of that explicitly. The home figured it out from your devices and your habits.

Hub is the same idea, for your digital life.

You bring in what you already have — your bot, your site, your ChatGPT and Claude accounts, your API keys, your scattered tools. Hub sees them all in one place, learns what you're trying to do, and starts making them cooperate: your bot drafts answers using your Claude account, your site posts updates to your channel, your APIs feed your dashboards. You don't write integrations. You describe what you want, in plain language.

And because Hub starts inside Telegram, all of this happens in one chat. No dashboards. No admin panels. No separate logins.
